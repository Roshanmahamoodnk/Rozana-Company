From coconut to value-added products

Building a Kerala coconut business

Rozana Company is a coconut value-chain website focused on premium food products, byproducts, and sustainable processing. The goal is to buy coconuts locally, process them efficiently, and sell higher-value coconut products for Kerala, India, and export markets.

Process

How the business works

1

Buy and grade

Source mature and tender coconuts from farmers, traders, and local aggregation points.

2

Process

Clean, split, extract, grate, press, dry, or filter based on the product line.

3

Pack

Use bottles, pouches, cartons, chilled packs, or industrial packs depending on shelf life.

4

Sell

Target retail, horeca, export, quick commerce, and byproduct buyers.

Why Kerala works

  • Cultural trust: coconut is already part of everyday food and tradition.
  • Supply base: local coconuts are widely available in Kerala and nearby regions.
  • Market need: ready-to-use coconut products save time for households and hotels.

Competitive angle

  • Premium brand: move from raw coconut to packaged coconut products.
  • Multiple revenue streams: food, beverage, and byproducts together.
  • Cleaner margins: better value per coconut by using the whole fruit.
